Questions

Before you book

Why are there two payments instead of one bundled price?

So it's clear what you're paying for. The Prosper fee covers clinical time and interpretation. The Lola Health fee covers the laboratory work itself. Bundling these into one number would make it harder to see the actual value of the consultation.

What if my results come back outside the normal range?

Any results requiring medical attention are always discussed directly with you and referred to your GP for follow-up. Prosper does not manage or treat abnormal results outside of a full clinical consultation with complete medical history.

How far in advance should I book my blood test?

At least 14 days before your consultation. This gives time for your home venepuncture appointment, lab processing, and for Dr Collins to review your results properly before you meet.

Is this a replacement for seeing my GP?

No. Prosper is a complementary service focused on performance and longevity assessment. It works alongside your existing GP care, not instead of it.

Will my coach see my results?

Only if you explicitly consent. If you do, your coach receives a short, practical summary with no sensitive medical information — just what's useful for your training.
