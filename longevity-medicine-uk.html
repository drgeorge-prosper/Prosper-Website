What it means at Prosper

Longevity medicine, defined properly.

Longevity medicine looks beyond a single symptom or a single test. It considers your cardiovascular and metabolic risk, hormonal health, body composition and recovery capacity together, then builds a plan around how you want to feel, perform and age.

  • Professionals and founders whose demanding careers leave little time for their own health

Questions

Frequently asked.

Is this the same as a private GP service?

No. Prosper is a longevity and performance programme built around baseline testing, medical interpretation and structured follow-up. It complements your GP rather than replacing primary care.

Do I need to be based near a specific city?

No. Prosper supports clients across the UK. Consultations are arranged directly with Prosper following onboarding, and the format is explained during your discovery call.

What does Prosper Clarity include?

Comprehensive baseline testing, a 60-minute doctor-led consultation, an individual health and treatment plan, an 8 to 12 week implementation period and a 45-minute progress review.

How is this different from an at-home testing kit?

A testing kit gives you numbers. Prosper gives you a doctor who reads those numbers alongside your history and goals, then builds and reviews a plan with you.
