3

Secure onboarding

If we both decide to proceed, you complete a secure clinical questionnaire and consent, away from the public website, and the programme fee is confirmed with all expected costs set out clearly.

4

Baseline testing

One appropriate baseline blood panel, completed close to home before your first consultation, so the conversation starts informed rather than speculative.

5

The initial consultation

Sixty minutes with Dr George. Your history, goals, results and life, considered together. A deep clinical review of you, not a walkthrough of numbers.

6

Your Clarity Plan

A written, personal plan: clear priorities, honest recommendations, and any further testing divided into recommended and genuinely optional, with costs explained before you commit to anything.

7

Implementation

Roughly eight to twelve weeks of putting the plan to work, completing any agreed investigations along the way.

8

Progress review

Forty-five minutes to review what changed: symptoms, energy, recovery, results. You leave with an updated plan and a clear recommendation for what comes next.

Testing philosophy

Testing, chosen properly.

Prosper does not sell unrestricted testing. Investigations are selected by Dr George, linked to your goals and clinical context, and split honestly into recommended and optional. You will always know what something costs and why it is suggested before you pay any provider.
