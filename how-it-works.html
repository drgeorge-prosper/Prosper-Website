How it works

A considered process, from first conversation to clear plan.

No test menus, no guesswork. One programme, properly sequenced.

Nine steps. Tap any step for more detail.

1

Apply

A short, non-clinical application.
Two minutes, no medical detail needed at this stage.
2

Discovery call

A complimentary 15-minute conversation with Dr George.
About what you're looking for and whether Prosper is the right fit. Not a medical consultation, and no obligation.
3

Secure onboarding

A secure questionnaire, consent and confirmed fee.
Completed privately away from the public website, with every expected cost set out clearly first.
4

Baseline testing

One baseline blood panel, close to home.
Completed before your consultation, so the conversation starts informed rather than speculative.
5

The consultation

Sixty minutes with Dr George.
Your history, goals, results and life, considered together as one connected picture, not a walkthrough of numbers.
6

Your Clarity Plan

A written, personal plan with clear priorities.
  • Honest, individual recommendations
  • Further testing split into recommended and optional
  • Every cost explained before you commit
7

Implementation

Eight to twelve weeks of putting the plan to work.
Completing any agreed investigations along the way.
8

Progress review

Forty-five minutes to review what changed.
Symptoms, energy, recovery and results, with an updated plan to take away.
9

What comes next

A clear recommendation for what follows.
An annual reassessment, or ongoing quarterly or six-monthly care through Prosper Continuum.

Testing philosophy

Testing, chosen properly.

Prosper does not sell unrestricted testing. Investigations are selected by Dr George, linked to your goals and clinical context, and split honestly into recommended and optional. You will always know what something costs and why it is suggested before you pay any provider.

The initial consultation

A full hour, in person with you.

This is where your baseline results, your history and your goals come together. Not a walkthrough of numbers, a conversation about what they mean for you and what happens next.
