How it works

From booking to
report in six steps.

A simple, clearly sequenced process from the moment you book to the moment you have your full clinical picture in hand.

1

Book and pay

Choose your package and pay the Prosper consultation fee securely via Stripe. You'll receive an instant confirmation email.

2

Order your blood panel

Your confirmation email includes a Lola Health link. Use it to order your 70-biomarker panel and book a home venepuncture appointment at a time that suits you.

Book at least 14 days before your consultation
3

Home venepuncture

A qualified phlebotomist visits you at home to take your blood sample. No clinic visit required.

4

Results processed

Your results return to Lola Health within 2 to 3 working days of your blood draw. Dr George reviews them fully before your consultation.

5

Your consultation

A one-hour clinical consultation with Dr George, conducted remotely. Covers your longevity markers, performance signals, recovery capacity, and any questions you have.

6

Report delivered

A full written report, your personalised action plan, and supplement and lifestyle recommendations, delivered within 48 hours of your consultation.

Every consultation is conducted personally by Dr George.

If you were referred by a coach

Your coach can receive a summary. Only if you say so.

With your explicit consent, we send a short, practical summary to your coach after each consultation. It contains no sensitive medical information, only what's relevant to your training: recovery capacity, fuelling considerations, and any adjustments worth making. Your full clinical report always remains confidential to you.
